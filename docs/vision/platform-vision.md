# J4MB Platform Vision

## Overview

J4MB is a cloud-native business platform focused on providing modular microservices for micro and small business systems.

The platform aims to provide reusable, scalable, and event-driven services covering:

- Financial operations
- Document management
- Workflow automation
- Notifications
- Inventory management
- distributed systems experimentation

----

## Objectives

- Build production-oriented engineering systems
- Explore modern distributed architecture
- Establish reusable platform services
- Practice scalable microservices patterns
- Create a public engineering portfolio

---

## Architectural Principles

- API-first design
- Domain-driven thinking
- Event-driven communication
- Cloud-native deployment
- Observability by design
- Modular service boundaries
